Lucky Roll Dice Game

Fancy your luck?

  1. Select a number of dice to roll
  2. Guess if the next roll will be higher or lower than the previous roll
  3. Each correct answer gives you a point to your current streak
  4. If you get it wrong you will lose your streak
  5. Click reset to change the number of dice
  6. Try and get the highest streak!
